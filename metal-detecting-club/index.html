About Treasure Seekers Club

Treasure Seekers Club is a community of metal detecting enthusiasts. Founded in 2024, our mission is to explore, discover, and preserve history. We welcome detectorists of all levels to join our hunts and share their discoveries.

Our Discoveries

A determined search through a freshly harvested field, with stubble left behind. While not the ideal conditions, we take what we can get—permissions can be hard to come by. Every field holds potential, and the thrill of the hunt keeps us going, even when the terrain is challenging.

A stunning display of history—these silver hammered coins date back to centuries past, each one telling a story of trade, power, and craftsmanship. Found during a recent hunt, they are a testament to the rich heritage waiting to be unearthed

A moment of discovery captured perfectly. One of our members proudly holds up their latest find, examining it closely in the natural light. It's in these moments that the thrill of the hunt comes to life, as history is brought back into the present.

A rare and exciting find—a cache of ancient coins nestled inside a clay pot, buried for safekeeping centuries ago. The earth has preserved this small piece of history, and now, it has been brought to light once again by our dedicated club members.
